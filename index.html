My favorite Band

Opeth

It is a Swedish progressive metal band formed in 1990 in Stockholm by David Isberg, later joining its oldest member, Mikael Åkerfeldt. In his compositions he has influences from progressive rock, jazz, blues and acoustic guitars of classic and folk style.

Since the recording of their first album, they have released 13 studio albums, 3 box sets, and four live albums. A complete exception to his initial musical style are the albums Damnation (2003), Heritage (2011), Pale Communion (2014), Sorceress (2016) and In Cauda Venenum (2019), which show a style absolutely geared towards progressive rock. 4

Members:

Mikael Åkerfeldt : Guitars (1990-present), Vocals (1992-present), Bass (1997), Piano (1997, 2011), Mellotron (2005, 2011)

Martín Méndez : Bass (1997-present)

Fredrik Åkesson : Guitars, Vocals (backing) (2007-present)
